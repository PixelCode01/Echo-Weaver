Defend the core at the center of the screen by creating destructive sound waves. Enemies will spawn from the edges and try to reach the core. Use your waves to destroy them before they can damage the core!

üí° Pro Tip

The core pulses with a cyan glow. Keep it safe at all costs - if enemies reach it, the game is over!

üéØ Game Mechanics

Speed Limits

The game uses progressive speed limits to keep it manageable:

  • Score 0-100: Maximum 1.5x speed
  • Score 100-200: Maximum 2.0x speed
  • Score 200+: Full speed progression

Combo System

Destroying enemies quickly builds combos:

  • Combos increase damage and score
  • Higher combos fill the Fever Meter
  • Fever Mode provides massive damage boost

Echo Burst

Your ultimate ability with a cooldown:

  • Clears all enemies in a large radius
  • Has a cooldown timer
  • Perfect for emergency situations

üèÜ Scoring & Achievements

Score Multipliers

  • Base enemy kill: 1 point
  • Combo bonus: +10% per combo level
  • Critical hits: 2x damage and score
  • Boss enemies: 10 points

Achievements

  • Boss Slayer: Defeat a boss enemy
  • Combo Master: Achieve high combo streaks
  • Wave Master: Use all wave modes effectively
  • Survivor: Survive for extended periods
