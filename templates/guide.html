üéÆ Gameplay Overview

Your goal is to defend the glowing core in the center by creating powerful sound waves. Enemies will approach from all sides‚Äîuse your waves to destroy them before they reach the core!

üïπÔ∏è Controls

Mouse: Click and drag to draw a line, release to unleash a wave.
Touch: Tap and drag to draw a line, release to unleash a wave.
1, 2, 3: Switch between wave modes (Normal, Focused, Wide).
Triple-tap (mobile): Cycle through wave modes.
SPACE: Echo Burst (clears nearby enemies).
Double-tap core (mobile): Echo Burst.

Tip

On mobile, you must wait for the touch cooldown bar to fill before drawing again. The cooldown increases as your score rises.

üìä Progress Bars

  • Fever Meter (bottom left): Fills as you build combos by defeating enemies quickly. When full, you enter Fever Mode for extra power!
  • Echo Burst Cooldown (bottom left): Shows when you can use your Echo Burst again.
  • Touch Cooldown (mobile): Shows when you can draw the next wave. The bar fills up as you wait.

üåä Wave Modes

Normal: Balanced damage and width.
Focused: High damage, narrow width‚Äîgreat for tough enemies.
Wide: Lower damage, but hits a larger area‚Äîgreat for groups.

üèÜ Scoring, Combos & Achievements
