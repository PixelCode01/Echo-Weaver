Mission Brief

Echo Weaver is a survival arena where every line you draw becomes a destructive sound wave. Enemies converge on the crystalline core at the center of the arena—if they reach it, the run ends. Stay in motion, chain eliminations, and ride the rhythm of the battlefield for as long as you can.

Controls at a Glance

Desktop

  • Click & Drag: Sketch a path for your next wave, release to fire.
  • 1 / 2 / 3: Swap between Normal, Focused, and Wide wave modes.
  • Spacebar: Trigger Echo Burst—an emergency pulse that nukes nearby threats.
  • Esc or P: Pause to catch your breath and strategize.

Mobile & Tablet

  • Tap & Drag: Draw a wave path with your finger and release to detonate.
  • Triple Tap: Anywhere in the arena to cycle through wave modes.
  • Echo Burst Button: Tap the glowing burst icon near the HUD to clear space.
  • Cooldown Bar: The yellow bar next to the burst button shows when you can draw again. Higher scores lengthen the cooldown slightly.

Prime tip

Short, precise lines are faster to cast. Use them to pick off stragglers while your Echo Burst recharges.

Reading the HUD

Your interface keeps you one step ahead. Here’s how to glance-and-go:

Fever Meter

Located bottom-left. Every consecutive kill pumps the bar. Once full, you enter Fever Mode—waves hit harder, glow brighter, and feel incredible.

Echo Burst Cooldown

Cyan bar under the Fever Meter. When it fills, you can pop another defensive blast. Don’t waste it; burst when you’re boxed in.

Touch Cooldown

Visible on every device near the HUD. It shows how soon you can sketch your next wave. Keep an eye on it when the arena gets hectic.

Wave Modes

Swapping modes mid-fight is vital. Each one rewrites how your line becomes damage:

Normal

Balanced reach and damage. Default choice for most encounters.

Focused

Narrow beam, heavy punch. Aim at elites, shielded enemies, or bosses when you need raw power.

Wide

Stretches across the arena with lower damage per hit. Use it to soften swarms, then clean up with Normal or Burst.

Enemy Field Guide

The longer you survive, the stranger the opposition gets. Expect the mix below to escalate as your score rises:

Standard Hostiles

Basic, Zigzag, and Speedster units rush the core with predictable patterns. Practice tracing lines that clip multiple targets at once.

Durability Checks

Ghosts and Shielded enemies shrug off single hits. Focused mode or chain reactions from explosions finish them faster.

Disruptors

Teleporters blink away, Reflectors bounce waves back, and Vortex units pull your path off-course. Stay adaptive—short lines give you time to react.

Swarm Architects

Splitter, Swarm, and TimeBomber enemies flood the field with extra bodies or delayed explosions. Keep an Echo Burst in reserve.

Boss Threats

Massive health pools, unique attacks, and score jackpots. Expect them every few waves—switch between Focused mode and Burst to stay in control.

Power-Up Cache

Power-ups float into the arena at clutch moments. Tap or drag across them as fast as possible—each one lasts about five seconds unless noted.

Core Barrier

Makes the core invulnerable for a brief window. Perfect during swarms or boss phases.

Wave Boost

Pumps damage and speed. Pair with Focused mode to shred elites.

Slow Time

Slows enemies to a crawl. Ideal chance to reposition and stack combos.

Echo Nova

Instantly wipes the board. Use it when your burst is down and the screen is chaos.

Wide Band

Inflates wave width. Great for securing pick-ups and catching flankers.

Time Stop

Freezes the field outright. Stack waves while enemies are frozen to create huge chains.

Wave Magnet

Pulls enemies into your lines. Combine with Wide mode to sweep entire clusters.

Chain Reaction

Turns every explosion into a cascade. Drop lines through clustered foes for fireworks.

Multi Wave

Instantly fires several waves. Use it the moment you pick it up for maximum coverage.

Score, Combos & Achievements

Points are only part of the high-score story—style matters too.

  • Combos: Every enemy defeated adds to your chain. Drop a wave too late and the combo decays, so keep lines short and frequent.
  • Fever Mode: Triggered at full combo charge. Expect a double-damage surge and an electric glow over the arena.
  • Critical Hits: Random spikes of extra damage keep scores rising; chain reaction waves boost your odds.
  • Achievements: Boss Slayer, Combo Master, Wave Master, and Survivor award bonus flair on the leaderboard.

Battlefield Wisdom

  • Draw diagonals that intersect multiple approach lanes—each wave can wipe more than one threat.
  • Stagger Echo Burst and power-up usage. You always want one escape tool cooling down, not both.
  • Rotate wave modes often. Starting a swarm with Wide mode and finishing with Focused prevents overkill waste.
  • Keep moving your pointer or finger; standing still encourages enemies to cluster on the core.
  • Boss waves reward patience. Watch for telegraphed attacks, then carve through exposed weak points.
